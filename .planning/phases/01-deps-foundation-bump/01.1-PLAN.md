## Why the AGP-9 transition was dropped (Option A from `01.1-SUMMARY.md`)

The plan target was "Hilt 2.57.1" — that version **does not exist on Maven Central**. The
latest published Hilt is `2.56.2`, which still relies on the AGP `BaseExtension` API removed
in AGP 9.2 and fails at plugin-apply time with `IllegalStateException: Android BaseExtension
not found`. No published Hilt currently supports AGP 9. The entire AGP-9 transition (Gradle
wrapper bump, AGP plugin bump, `compileSdk`/`targetSdk` 36, and Media3 1.10) is therefore
deferred. Slopper stays on **Gradle 8.11.1 / AGP 8.7.3 / compileSdk 35** for the remainder of
Phase 1.

This forces **Media3 (DEPS-10) into CASE B**: keep `media3 = "1.9.1"` + `nextlibMedia3Ext = "1.9.1-0.11.0"` with a dated deferral comment. The 1.10.0 path is also gated on `compileSdk = 36`.
